## Elicitation of latent capabilities

### Why this topic exists
- Track work on “elicitation” (prompting, fine-tuning, partial-parameter tuning) as a lens on what models already contain vs what training teaches.

### Key questions
- How many parameters / bits of update are needed to recover capability on a task?
- How do results vary by model family, size, and task format?
- What’s the safety implication: capability access, evaluation, and deployment risk?
