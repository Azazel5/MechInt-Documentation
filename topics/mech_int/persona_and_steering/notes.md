## Persona and steering

### Why this topic exists
- Track work on identifying, monitoring, and intervening on latent traits or behaviors (e.g. “personas”) via activation-space directions, representation engineering, or other steering methods.

### Key questions
- What is being measured (trait, policy, preference, refusal style) and how stable is it?
- How do interventions generalize across tasks, prompts, and domains?
- How do these methods interact with fine-tuning and post-training?
